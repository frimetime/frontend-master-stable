Step 1

Go to the top right corner and go to your friendlist.

Step 2

Find some friends to frime with!

Search for them or invite them to FRIMETIME!

Step 3

Now you can start a Frime.

You need a title, description, starting time, date and the number of guests!

Step 4

Look at your feed, as you can see created Frimes by yourself and from your friends!

Join other Frimes with a tap on an active Frime.

Step 5

Now if there are more than 2 people in a Frime you can chat with each other!

After the Frime is over, the chat will be deleted also.
